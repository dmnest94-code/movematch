Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

This is a practical starter policy for the MoveMatch MVP and should be reviewed before large-scale advertising or expansion.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, moving date, pickup and destination ZIP codes, move details, service preferences, and information about special items or access conditions.

How we use information

We use this information to review your request, recommend an appropriate moving plan, contact you, and connect you with a licensed moving service provider when appropriate.

Sharing with service providers

We may share relevant request information with licensed moving companies and providers involved in preparing an estimate or completing your move. We do not sell your personal information as a standalone marketing list.

Calls, texts and email

When you expressly agree through the form, MoveMatch may contact you by telephone, text message or email regarding your moving request. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchasing services.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact MoveMatch using the contact information published on the main website.
